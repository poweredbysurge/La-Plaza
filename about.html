For an authentic Southern California experience, plan a day at La Plaza in La Jolla. We offer the amenities and services to turn ordinary weekends into exceptional ones.

Start the day with a rich gourmet espresso and pamper yourself at an upscale, organic salon. Then, enjoy lunch with unparalleled ocean views before spending the afternoon browsing unique San Diego luxury shopping.

Whether you live nearby or are just visiting, La Plaza provides convenient luxury for a day of embracing everything you love about coastal living.
